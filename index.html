Our Story

On May 2 2017, Dr. Herman Duckie had a dream of creating the perfect bath toy, and since that day, bathtime has never been the same.

Here at Rubber Ducks Unlimited, we only use the finest rubber for our duckies, ensuring that your rubber duck will last for generations to come. We believe a rubber duck should be an investment, not just a purchase, and the quality of our ducks reflects that.
